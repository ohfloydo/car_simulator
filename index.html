Car Trace Simulation

Car Length: px   Car Width: px  

Speed: 0 px/frame

Instructions:

  • Click on the canvas to place the car.
  • Hold Shift and click to place obstacles.
  • Hold Alt and click and drag to draw reference lines.
  • Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to steer.
  • Use the Up and Down Arrow keys to control speed.
  • Adjust speed using the scrollbar.
  • Click the Rotate 90° Clockwise button to rotate the car.
